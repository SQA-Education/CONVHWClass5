1. Conversation Awareness

Professional Networking in the U.S.

In the U.S., many opportunities come through networking, not only formal applications. Professionals are expected to introduce themselves clearly and explain their qualifications with confidence.

A recruiter phone screen is often brief, but it matters a lot. Candidates need to summarize their background, respond to common questions, and end the conversation professionally.

Networking events are not only about collecting contacts. They also require active listening, appropriate formality, and meaningful follow-up.
